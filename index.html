About me

I'm a Lead Software Engineer, Product Owner, and Cloud + Technical Architecture enthusiast. I currently lead the data strategy team at a Fortune 50 company. Problem solver both at heart and by choice.

I love using a variety of technologies to solve business problems, as well as develop and lead software teams. When I am not doing that, I write How-to tutorials, and dabble around with different AWS Services.

With about a decade of trial and error, I share what good or bad technical architecture is, building effective teams, how to handle multiprocessing in Python, how to excel as an individual contributor as well as a tech lead, and how to write secure code. Subscribe to my Newsletter.
